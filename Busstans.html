Bus stands

  • Bus stand is a designated parking location where a buses waits out of service between scheduled public transport services.
  • Bus station is a structure where city or intercity buses stop to pick up and drop off passengers.
  • Bus terminus Bus terminus is a designated place where a bus or coach starts or ends its scheduled route. The terminus is the designated place that a timetable is timed from. For larger bus Schedules.
  • Bus depot Bus depot can also be used to refer to a bus station, it generally refers to a bus garage.
  • Bus stop, which is usually simply a place on the roadside, where buses can stop.
