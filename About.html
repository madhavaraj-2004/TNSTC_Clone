Genesis of State Transport Undertakings

  • On 01.01.1972, Pallavan Transport Corporation was established under Companies Act, 1956 and buses operated in Chennai and Chengalpattu were brought under it and paved way for inception of other State Transport Undertakings.
  • Government owned public transport bus operator in Tamil Nadu, India. It operates Intercity bus services to cities within Tamil Nadu, and from Tamil Nadu to its neighbouring states.   • It operates Intercity or Moffusil bus services to cities in Tamil Nadu and to neighbouring states.
