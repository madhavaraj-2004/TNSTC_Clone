Future Vision

  • To make bus transportation user friendly, affordable, efficient and provide last mile connectivity;
  • To provide bus travel with alternate fuel such as Electric, CNG, LNG etc;
  • Introduction of buses enabling easy access for Differently Abled persons;
  • Use of IT, ITS for online monitoring of buses & real-time passenger info;
  • To make easy and safe door-to-door journeys;
  • Convenient ticketing with simple digital payment;
  • To attain the sustainable development goals by 2030.

Fare Concessions

  • MPs / MLAs
  • Accredited Journalists
  • Freedom Fighters & their Legal Heirs
  • Cancer Patients & Differently-abled persons
  • Drama Artists, HIV/AIDS patients, Senior Citizen Pass

Concessions for Students

  • Students 1st Std to 12th Std in Govt recognized schools
  • Govt ITI, Arts & Science, and Polytechnic students eligible
  • Valid for Ordinary, Express & Deluxe buses
  • 50% concession for private colleges & polytechnics
