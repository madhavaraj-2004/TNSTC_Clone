Institute of Road Transport

The aim of the Institute is to disseminate knowledge on various aspects of technology developments and transport management to the officers and supervisory staff of State Transport Undertakings in Tamil Nadu by organizing training courses, seminars, workshops and circulation of journals.

Purpose

  • To improve bus transportation by adopting new technologies and developments
  • To educate and train staff and officers of the TNSTC
  • To promote technology-oriented education in road transport
  • To conduct scientific and operational research on road transport

Training

  • Training for light motor vehicles, two-wheelers, and heavy vehicles
  • Advanced heavy transport vehicle driver training
  • Training offered at IRT Chennai and Transport Corporations headquarters
  • Programs on Engineering, Management, Finance, Materials, Transportation, Computerisation

Management

  • Managed by a Governing Council
  • The Secretary to Government, Transport Department, Tamil Nadu is the Chairman
  • The Director of IRT is the Member Secretary of the Council
  • Ensures training and education for all categories of STU staff and officers
