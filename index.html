Welcome to TNSTC

Tamil Nadu State Transport Corporation Limited (TNSTC) is a public transport bus operator in Tamil Nadu, India. It operates Intercity or Moffusil bus services to cities in Tamil Nadu, and to neighbouring states.

Top Destinations

Tamil Nadu State Transport Corporation (TNSTC) operates buses to various destinations across Tamil Nadu and neighboring states. Some of the top destinations covered by TNSTC includes express, deluxe, and ultra deluxe buses, ensuring connectivity to major cities, towns, and rural areas. Popular Bus Stands In Tamilnadu

If you are traveling through Tamil Nadu by bus, you will likely come across major bus stands such as Chennai CMBT (Koyambedu) Bus Stand, Kilambakkam Bus Stand, Salem Bus Stand, Erode Bus Stand, Coimbatore (Covai) Bus Stand, Madurai Bus Stand, and Trichy Bus Stand. These bus terminals serve as key transportation hubs, connecting cities and towns across the state with frequent bus services, making travel convenient and accessible for passengers.
